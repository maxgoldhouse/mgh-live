This well kept seaview dream villa located in a prime location of the greater Villamartin Golf area offers everything as a holiday home or for your new permanent residence in the sunny garden of the southern Costa Blanca. The garden is well designed and nicely arranged with its plants, and is easy to maintain with automatic irrigation. The villa has a large dining-living area with open fireplace and cozy seating and a small bar area. The open kitchen with breakfast bar and adjoining utility room gives you the necessary space to invite your guests. The private pool and the conservatory get sun from morning until the evening. The outdoor area is tiled, spacious and also offers an outdoor shower and a pool house. In the basement there is plenty of space for all your sports clothing, or set up a Spanish "Bodega". The villa has 3 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, a large terrace on the top floor with an unobstructed panoramic views to the salt lake, the sea and the mountains. With numerous golf courses, restaurants, marinas, long walks on the white sandy beaches, swimming in the sea, all this is to discover. The villa is offered fully furnished.
