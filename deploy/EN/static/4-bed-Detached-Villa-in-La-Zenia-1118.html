In La Zenia, part of Orihuela Costa, you will enjoy the warm climate and over 250 days of sunshine a year. The sea front of La Zenia has promenades along the sea, full marina infrastructure and all kinds of water sports. This is a great place to enjoy sports, adventure tourism, as well as some of the best golf courses on the Mediterranean (Villamartin Golf, Las Ramblas Golf and Campoamor Golf). All the beaches of Orihulea Costa have blue flags and are well kept and clean. Enjoy the cozy sandy coves, beautiful cliffs, and quiet bays. A two storey furnished villa in the Spanish classic "rustic style" with a view to the sea offers living room with fireplace, equipped kitchen, dining room, 3 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms and on the 1st floor a bedroom on a large solarium with panoramic view overlooking the sea. On the ground floor there is a gym with sauna, library with Spanish wine bodega, utility room, a 2 car garage and 2 parking spaces in front. The plot is 700 sqm incl. a lovely Spanish garden, recreation area with barbecue and the private pool.
