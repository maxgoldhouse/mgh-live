This semi detached house is situated in the prestigious residential area of Campoamor Beach. Here the beach-front living comes combined with absolute tranquility, privacy and sea views. The layout of the house is very practical and includes the conservatory dining room, living room with open fireplace and American kitchen and additional dining area. Then we have one bedroom, guest toilet, utility room. On the upper floor are two further bedrooms and 1 bathroom plus a wonderful south-facing terrace to relax on.
