Our Tradition - It's all about you

MaxGoldHouse is a traditional Estate Agent and operates on that basis. Our focus is on the property and the buyers needs. We have been operating in this way for more than 20 years and continue to thrive with a long list of happy buyers enjoying their Spanish holiday and retirement homes.

Our Knowledge - Your Requirements

We want our buyers to feel comfortable in the knowledge that the whole process of buying a home with MaxGoldHouse is totally transparent. We can offer assistance throughout the whole purchasing process from arranging viewings, that match with your exact requirements, to making an offer to the vendor. We will advise you on engaging a local solicitor. We can assist you with the process of opening a bank account with a local bank and getting your Spanish identity cards(NIE).

A Shared Burden

For many of our customers travelling to Spain to view homes is an extra financial burden that they would not have if they were viewing property in their home town. In light of this we will refund up to €150 to you when you buy with maxgoldhouse. You can use this to offset the costs of accommodation while you are viewing property with us. We only ask that you allow us to organise a 3 day viewing trip to allow you to see a full range of property on offer and give you enough time and space to make decision that you are completely happy with.
