This cosy villa is surrounded by 4 golf courses and has been completely renovated with great attention to detail and offers typical Spanish flair. The villa is all on one level and has a beautifully furnished separate guest house with 1 bedroom and 1 bath, also a large jacuzzi all surrounded by a high wall which gives a very interesting garden space with privacy. The southwest facing plot offers plenty of space for free relaxation and there is enough space to build a private pool. The villa offers a spacious living room with open kitchen, (approx 45 sqm), 2 bedrooms, 1 bathroom, storage, and a sunny roof terrace (solarium) with an extra football-tv room with a real feel-good atmosphere. The future owner will enjoy the mild summer evenings on the terrace in front of a rustic outdoor grill. It is being sold unfurnished. The four golf courses in the immediate vicinity are just minutes away, also the new luxury shopping centre with 160 different shops and - not to forget - the wonderful beaches and the Mediterranean just a few minutes by car.
