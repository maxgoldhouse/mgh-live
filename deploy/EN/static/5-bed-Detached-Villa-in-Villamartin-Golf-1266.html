A unique opportunity. A beautiful finca style villa at the centre of Villamartin golf and Campoamor golf. The house and plot offer complete and perfect privacy, surrounded by a high wall with garage and carport and in the midst of a beautifully landscaped mediterranean garden. A large new pool, plenty of romantic corners, large-scale terraces, surrounded by artificial grass and a separate children's playground. The swimming pool, sunbathing terraces and rustic barbecue area are ideal for relaxing hours. Through the conservatory, with extra chill out space leads the way into the living room and dining room with open fireplace and access into the kitchen, followed by a utility room. There are 3 bedrooms with fitted wardrobes, 2 bathrooms all on one level. The solarium is accessible from the garden and enjoy a panoramic view. Another advantage is the separate garden guest house with 2 bedrooms and 1 bathroom - as well as the garage. A carport for 2 cars round this property perfectly. The perfect location of this charming object does the rest. The villa is offered for sale unfurnished and offers space for your own creative ideas.
