Modern detached villas located in Daya Vieja - a lovely Spanish village located next to Almoradi, Rojales and Ciudad Quesada. Daya Vieja offers a wide range of shops, bars and restaurants and is a short drive to the beautiful beaches of Guardamar. The villas consist of 3 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, 1 toilet, open living/dining room and open kitchen. Leading from the living area is a terrace which looks onto the beautiful private pool. There is also a terrace leading from the upstairs master bedrooms, and there is an additional option of having a 61m2 solarium. Other benefits include a low maintenance garden, off road parking and pre installed air con.
