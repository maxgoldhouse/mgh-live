New to the market! This incredibly spacious & bright detached country property has been nicely situated on the outskirts of Daya Nueva - a lovely Spanish village next to Almoradi. There are 4 bedrooms, 3 bathrooms (1 en-suite), independent kitchen and utility room, summer kitchen, attic room & 150 m2 garage. There is an abundance of outside space including terraces, a solarium and a huge garden area. The plot has been utilised as roughly half having been planted with palms, the other half well maintained gardens - although the ample plot could easily lend itself to a variety of purposes. Benefits include central heating, parking, mosquito blinds and alarm system. Golf & main towns are very close by and the blue flag beaches of Guardamar del Segura are only a 10 minutes drive away. Well worth viewing for those looking for an authentic detached Spanish home. Arrange a viewing with Maxgoldhouse!
